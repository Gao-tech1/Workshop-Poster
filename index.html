Workshop Overview

Intelligent Vehicles (IVs) are central to improving traffic efficiency and enabling smart cities. Despite strong progress, performance in dense, complex urban settings remains challenging.

This workshop advances safety-certifiable navigation and controls to close these gaps and ensure reliable IV operation in real-world cities.
